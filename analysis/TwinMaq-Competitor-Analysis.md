## Competitive Positioning Summary

**TwinMaq's key differentiators vs. Vancore:**
1. **Materials & Finishes Selector** — buyers can customize finishes inside the platform
2. **Marketing Content Package** — social media videos, animations included as add-ons
3. **Arabic-first** — designed for Saudi/MENA market natively
4. **Hosted platform** — not just a deliverable file, but a live platform with updates
5. **Design Zone credibility** — engineering firm with offices in SA, JO, EG — local presence

**Vancore's strengths to counter:**
- UAE-based, well-designed brand
- Strong feature set, clear proposal structure
- Professional payment terms

**Our counter-strategy:**
- Lead with local presence (Saudi office = no time zone issue, VAT-compliant)
- Add marketing content as differentiator (they don't offer this)
- Offer flexible pricing with modular add-ons
- Highlight materials & finishes as unique feature
